Bio

I’m Kéra Matthews, a journalism and marketing student at the University of Maryland with experience in reporting, editing and multimedia storytelling. I grew up in Prince George's County and later Boxborough, Massachusetts, and I have loved writing, reading and other forms of creativity all of my life.

My work spans campus news, culture writing and short-form video. I enjoy creating and discussing work that feels thoughtful and human, whether that’s through articles, video packages or digital media.

I’m especially interested in media and entertainment. I hope to work in book publishing, exploring all parts of the business and eventually marketing stories from authors of color. Through my experiences in both majors, I plan to apply those skills to the future company I contribute to or lead, helping bring diverse books to young readers.

What I Cover

My interests include culture, student life, community storytelling and multimedia reporting. I’m drawn to stories that balance strong reporting with a clear narrative voice. My other general interests include books and other forms of media, particularly entertainment and pop culture.
